Services

Custom Shopify Theme Development

Whether it's from scratch or modifying an existing theme, I ensure a pixel-perfect design, responsive layout, and seamless user experience.
02

Shopify SEO

Boost your Shopify store’s visibility with expert SEO strategies. I implement on-page SEO, technical SEO, keyword optimization, site speed improvements, structured data, and backlinks to ensure higher rankings, increased traffic, and improved conversions.
03

Shopify Store Migration

Seamlessly migrate your online store from WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce, Wix, WordPress, or any other platform to Shopify without losing data. I ensure a hassle-free transfer of products, customer information, order history, and SEO rankings while enhancing store performance.
04

Shopify Store Setup & Customization

Get a fully functional, professionally designed Shopify store tailored to your business. I handle theme setup, app integrations, payment gateway configuration, navigation setup, branding, and design customization to create a high-converting and user-friendly storefront.
05

Shopify Speed Optimization

Speed up your Shopify store for faster load times, better user experience, and improved SEO. I optimize images, minify code, implement lazy loading, remove unnecessary scripts, and leverage caching to ensure lightning-fast performance.
06

Shopify UI/UX Design & Optimization

Enhance your store’s user experience with intuitive navigation, stunning visuals, and high-converting designs. I focus on conversion rate optimization (CRO), mobile responsiveness, A/B testing, and modern UI/UX principles to maximize engagement and sales.
07

Figma to Shopify Liquid Conversion

Turn your Figma designs into a fully functional Shopify store using custom Liquid coding. I ensure pixel-perfect implementation, responsiveness, and Shopify compatibility while maintaining seamless performance and customization options.
08

Figma to GemPages Page Builder

Bring your Figma designs to life with GemPages, one of Shopify’s best drag-and-drop page builders. I create high-converting landing pages, sales funnels, and custom sections with precise design accuracy, speed, and performance optimization.
09

Figma to PageFly Page Builder

Transform your Figma mockups into a fully optimized PageFly landing page. I build visually stunning, responsive, and conversion-focused pages using PageFly’s advanced customization features to enhance store performance and increase sales.
10

Shopify Marketing (FB, IG, TikTok, Email, Google Ads)

Drive traffic and skyrocket sales with data-driven Shopify marketing strategies. I specialize in Facebook Ads, Instagram Ads, TikTok Ads, Google Ads, influencer marketing, email marketing, and automation to maximize ROI and scale your eCommerce business.
